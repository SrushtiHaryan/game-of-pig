Instructions to Play*

This is a very simple yet fun jeopardy dice game!🎲

👉For every turn, a player repeatedly rolls a die until either a 1 is rolled or the player holds their turn.

👉At any time during a player's turn, the player is faced with two decisions:

👉1. Keep Rolling: If the player rolls number other than 1, the current score increases by that number

but as soon as the player rolls 1, the current score is reset.

👉2. Hold: If the player holds, the current score gets added up to the total score of that player and its the opponent's turn.

👉The winner is the player that reaches score of 💯 first.

Esc to continue.
